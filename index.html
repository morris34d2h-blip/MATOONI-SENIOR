What's Happening at Matooni

🏠
Boarding Section Now Open!

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our new Boarding Section! We currently have 11 students already settled in and we warmly welcome more students to join our growing boarding community. A safe, structured, and supportive home away from home.

🎉 11 Students Enrolled — Join Us!
💻
New Subjects for Grade 10

Exciting news for our Grade 10 students! We have introduced two brand-new subjects designed to broaden skills and prepare students for the modern world:

  • 🖥️ Computer Studies — Digital literacy & technology skills
  • 🍳 Home Science — Practical life & household management
🏆
Sports — This Term

Under the energetic leadership of Mr. Mutinda, our dedicated Games Teacher, the school is gearing up for an action-packed term on the sporting front! Students are training hard and competing across:

  • ⚽ Soccer — Boys & Girls teams
  • 🏐 Volleyball — Boys & Girls squads
  • 🎯 Indoor Games — Sharp minds, steady hands

Mr. Mutinda is committed to nurturing talent, building teamwork, and instilling a winning spirit in every student — prepared not just to compete, but to excel!

🎓
Academic Day — A Huge Success!

Last Wednesday, Matooni Senior School held its highly anticipated Academic Day and it was a tremendous success! Students, teachers, parents, and the entire school community came together to celebrate academic excellence and achievement. It was a proud and memorable moment for our entire school family — a true testament to the hard work and dedication of our learners and staff.

Academic Day celebration — Matooni Senior School

Upcoming Trip — Machakos ASK Show
